Top Attractions in Hyderabad

From historical monuments to modern marvels, explore the best of what Hyderabad has to offer.

Charminar

The iconic monument built in 1591 CE is the symbol of Hyderabad. The 56m tall structure features four grand arches and minarets.

Learn More

Golconda Fort

This magnificent fort dates back to the 13th century and showcases stunning examples of military architecture. Don't miss the evening sound and light show!

Learn More

Ramoji Film City

One of the largest film studio complexes in the world, Ramoji Film City offers tours, attractions, and a glimpse into the world of cinema.

Learn More

Hussain Sagar

This heart-shaped lake built in 1563 features a giant monolithic statue of Buddha in the center. Enjoy boating or the surrounding Necklace Road.
