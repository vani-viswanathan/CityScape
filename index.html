Explore the local Cuisine

Hyderabadi Biryani

A flavorful mix of basmati rice, aromatic spices, and tender meat, cooked in the dum pukht style.

Try at: Paradise, Bawarchi, Shah Ghouse

Haleem

A stew made with meat, lentils, wheat, and spices, slow-cooked to perfection. Popular during Ramadan.

Try at: Pista House, Café 555, Shah Ghouse
